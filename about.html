Company

Xthrill Adventure Academy, Lavasa was established on 01 Dec 2008. With an aim of providing quality services in the field of Adventure. Set against a stunning backdrop of the green and rugged terrain of the Sahyadris and a soaring uninterrupted horizon, XTHRILL ADVENTURES is located within the sprawling expanse of the hill city of Lavasa. Spread across 25000 acres amidst 7 hills and a 60 km lakefront, the city of Lavasa is situated at a 45 minute drive from Pune and a 3-hour drive from Mumbai. For those of you whose idea of entertainment veers off the beaten track, XTHRILL is the answer. Partnered by Z-bac, Xthrill brings together 100% unadulterated extreme adventure activities with the assurance of international safety standards. With the widest range of activities at any one place, there is something here for every age and taste. Whatever your preferred element of challenge, be it land, water or air, we open up the possibilities of a new and thrilling experience for you. Our Vision Our Vision is to provide unadulterated adventure sports with high quality of Outbound and Inbound Training for Corporates, Educational Institutions, Sports Team, Defence Establishment along with Family Adventure Holidays in natural environment of Forest.
